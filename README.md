## ✨ Key Features

### 🔢 Persistent Channel Numbers

Each channel keeps the same number forever — even after updates. Memorize your favorites and switch instantly.

### 🌍 Automatic “My Language” Scan

One click finds working channels in your native language (Portuguese, English, Spanish, French, German, +25 more). No more endless scrolling.

### ⭐ Offline & Error Handling

Dead channels are automatically removed and retested every 7 days. When they come back online, they reappear in your list — no duplicates.

### 💾 Favorites Manager

Star your go-to channels. They always stay at the top of the list, sorted exactly how you like.

### 📡 Smart Source Fetching

Aggregates from multiple IPTV-org sources (language, country, global, free-TV, API) with proxy fallbacks — nothing is left behind.

### 🕹️ Full Keyboard & Remote Control

Type numbers on the home screen, press **ENTER**. While watching: use arrows, **Page Up/Down**, or on-screen buttons. OSD shows channel info.

### 📱 Responsive Design

Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Touch-friendly controls auto-hide when idle.

### ⚡ HLS + Native Video Support

Plays `.m3u`, `.m3u8`, and direct streams via **HLS.js** or native video.
